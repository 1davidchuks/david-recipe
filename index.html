Spaghetti Aglio e Olio – quick, tasty, and classic

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Sauté sliced garlic in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes and cooked spaghetti to the pan.
  4. Toss everything together and garnish with parsley.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
